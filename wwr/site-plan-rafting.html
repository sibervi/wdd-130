Overview

Purpose

This White Water Rafting Company main goal is to help you to have a good time, or as we like to say we want you to have “an energy boost, and increase of connection with nature”, something that we are conscious is the necessity of safeness and respect (which are our tops priorities). We all know, especially nowadays, how hard it is to feel stuck in a routine, it is something that generates a huge amount of stress, and we definitely want to help adventures all over the world to break free from the stress of their routine, and safely gain a boost of energy. The rule number one for an awesome time with us is to like and respect nature we invest 15% of all our profits to protect and preserve the environment we are in, we also are super careful to preserve the animal life that surrounds us. Adventure on the water fights every day to be a trustworthy company where you and your family, and friends, and friends of friends, and so on can have a great adventure connecting with nature finding out new passions and stepping out of the comfort zone. As as we like to say: an energy boost and increasing of connection are waiting for you!

Audience

The Adventure on water is for everyone that wants to have fun and likes to be in contact with nature. The main thing to have a great time with us is first to be respectful, we prioritize respect among our employees (they will take care of your security and wellbeing), be respectful with nature is also expected of everyone that is thinking in get a ride with us in this adventure, and respect people regardless their color, religion, sexuality, age or special conditions. The second thing is to be open to trying it, many people are spectacle about their capabilities based on age, fitness, or fears, we invite everyone willing to try it, even if you think you are not capable of such a thing, you can get surprised with yourselves. We recommend you invite your family members, and friends to have a better experience, but you can have tunes of fun if you are alone with our team. If you are a photography lover we've got your back covered, so don't worry about that, we have an awesome photography team that will make sure to register all your memories. You are welcome and we are waiting for you to embark on this adventure on water.
